## Requirements
### Requirement: Contact page without Calendly scheduling
The contact page SHALL NOT contain Calendly scheduling integration to simplify contact functionality and remove external dependencies.

#### Scenario: Contact page loads without Calendly
- **WHEN** user navigates to the contact page
- **THEN** the page SHALL NOT display any Calendly scheduling buttons
- **AND** the page layout SHALL remain intact with email, phone, and location information
- **AND** all other contact methods SHALL function normally
